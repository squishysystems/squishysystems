About

Lauren Traugott-Campbell is a freelance graphic designer and artist based in New York City. She has worked with the Museum of Modern Art, Rhode Island School of Design, The Nation Institute, Jacobin Magazine, and Tribune Magazine. Her work has been featured in VICE, Fast Company, and magCulture, among others. She holds an MFA in Graphic Design from the Rhode Island School of Design.
