Pop Culture Color Theory Poster

Poster advertising James Goggin's "Pop Culture Color Theory" lecture at the RISD Museum. The poster was created by individually printing layers of cyan, magenta and yellow to create the color black, thus revealing how printers create colors. Each poster varies slightly based on the order of printing each color and the tiny imperfections that appear as the registration shifts.

Spring 2018.
