Student Action with Farmworkers Factsheet Series

A re-designed system (currently in production) for Student Action with Farmworkers' factsheets. The double-sided factsheets are used by the organization when educating the public about farmworker issues, as well as available online for download. The factsheets are printed on affordable copy paper of varying colors which allow them to be thematically distinguished from one another, as well be printed quickly and cheaply by the organization.

Winter 2018.
