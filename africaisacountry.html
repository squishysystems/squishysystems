Africa is a Country

Identity, print and web re-design for Africa is a Country following a merging with Jacobin Magazine. This identity needed to enhance AIAC's existing readership, while also fit within the existing design framework Jacobin Magazine. Along with making a logotype for Africa is a Country, the re-design also includes examples of how the magazine can, and plans to, expand to include future brands.

Summer 2016.
