Lauren Traugott-Campbell

Block Party

Typographic identity poster system for a hypothetical event at the Cooper Hewitt. Designed as a homage to the Theo van Doesburg's cover design for Mecano, Block Party is an event that celebrates the culture of typographic influence between De Stijl, Bauhaus and Russian Avant Garde movements. Adaptive to multiple dimensions, Block Party's design changes overall shape, as well as type content, as featured designers' names may be exchanged for "Block Party."

Spring 2016.
